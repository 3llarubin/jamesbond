James Bond: No time to die 2020

Bond has left his post and is enjoying a quiet life in Jamaica. However, it is short-lived when his old friend Felix Leiter from the CIA shows up and asks for help. The mission to rescue a kidnapped scientist turns out to be more treacherous than expected.

James Bond

Played by Daniel Craig

Daniel Wroughton Craig, born March 2, 1968 in Chester, Cheshire, is a British actor, known among other things as the sixth James Bond in the films Casino Royale, Quantum of Solace, Skyfall, Specter and No Time to Die.

Ana Celia de Armas Caso, born April 30, 1988 in Havana, Cuba, is a Cuban actress. She also holds Spanish citizenship. She made her acting debut in 2006 in the Spanish film Una rosa de Francia.
